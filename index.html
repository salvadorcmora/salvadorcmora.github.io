GT Movies Store

Overview: A movie catalog web app that lets users browse titles, view details, and simulate rent/purchase actions.

  • Homepage: This web application first opens up to the homepage, where you'll be able to locate many of the offerings GT Movie Store has.
  • Movies: GT Movies Store has a wide selection of films to choose from. Here, you'll be able to find them all.
  • User Creation: Users are able to create their own accounts to be able to review and purchase films.
  • Reviews: Being a film application, users are able to express their opinions about any film they choose. They can also edit and delete them as they'd like.
  • Purchases: GT Movies is also not just a film review application, as users are also able to purchase Blu-rays of their favorite films.

Below is a link to the website. It's completely free! Create an account and try it for yourself!
